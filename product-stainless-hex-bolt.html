Stainless Steel Hex Bolt DIN 933

Product Code: SS-HB-933-A2

Our premium stainless steel hex bolts are manufactured to DIN 933 standard with full threading. Made from high-quality AISI 304 (A2) stainless steel, these bolts offer excellent corrosion resistance and durability for a wide range of applications.

Fully threaded design provides maximum grip strength and versatility. Suitable for indoor and outdoor applications where corrosion resistance is required.

Material Grade AISI 304 (A2-70) / AISI 316 (A4-80)
Standard DIN 933 / ISO 4017
Size Range M3 to M36
Length Range 10mm to 300mm
Tensile Strength 700 MPa (A2-70) / 800 MPa (A4-80)
Thread Type Metric Coarse (Full Thread)
