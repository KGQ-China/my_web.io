Standard Fasteners

Overview

Standard fasteners form the backbone of modern manufacturing and construction. These are the most commonly used fasteners that conform to internationally recognized standards such as DIN, ISO, ANSI, and JIS. Our standard fasteners are manufactured to precise tolerances and undergo rigorous quality control to ensure consistent performance.

We stock an extensive inventory of standard fasteners in various materials, sizes, and finishes to meet immediate delivery requirements. From small machine screws to large structural bolts, we have the standard fasteners you need for any application.

Product Range

  • Hex Bolts & Screws (DIN 931/933)
  • Socket Head Cap Screws (DIN 912)
  • Machine Screws (DIN 84/85)
  • Hex Nuts (DIN 934)
