Copper & Brass Fasteners

Overview

Copper and brass fasteners offer unique properties that make them indispensable in specific industrial applications. With excellent thermal and electrical conductivity, superior corrosion resistance, and natural antimicrobial properties, these fasteners are the preferred choice for electrical, plumbing, and decorative applications.

Our copper and brass fasteners are manufactured from high-purity alloys, ensuring consistent performance and compliance with international standards including ASTM, DIN, and JIS specifications.

Material Grades

  • C11000 (ETP Copper)
  • C26000 (Cartridge Brass)
  • C36000 (Free Cutting Brass)
  • C46400 (Naval Brass)
  • C63000 (Aluminum Bronze)

Key Benefits

  • Excellent electrical conductivity
  • Superior thermal conductivity
  • Natural antimicrobial properties
  • Outstanding corrosion resistance
  • Attractive golden appearance

Technical Specifications

Conductivity 15-100% IACS
Tensile Strength 200-550 MPa
Hardness HRB 40-90
Melting Point 900-1083°C
Standards ASTM B16 / B98
Finish Polished / Natural
