Rivets

Overview

Rivets are permanent mechanical fasteners that have been used for centuries to join materials together. Unlike bolts and screws, rivets create a permanent joint that cannot be easily disassembled without destroying the fastener. This makes them ideal for applications requiring high structural integrity and vibration resistance.

We manufacture a comprehensive range of rivets including blind rivets (pop rivets), solid rivets, semi-tubular rivets, and structural rivets in various materials and sizes to meet diverse industrial needs.

Rivet Types

  • Open End Blind Rivets
  • Closed End Blind Rivets
  • Multi-Grip Rivets
  • Structural Rivets
  • Solid Rivets
  • Semi-Tubular Rivets

Key Benefits

  • Permanent secure fastening
  • Excellent vibration resistance
  • One-sided installation
  • No thread stripping issues
  • Lightweight solution
  • Cost-effective for high volume

Technical Specifications

Diameter Range 2.4mm - 12.7mm
Grip Range 0.5mm - 50mm
Head Styles Dome, Countersunk, Large Flange

Installation Guide

Proper rivet installation ensures maximum joint strength:

  • Select correct rivet diameter (typically 1.5x material thickness)
  • Ensure proper grip range for your material thickness
  • Use appropriate rivet gun or tool for the rivet size
  • Drill holes perpendicular to the surface
  • Maintain consistent pressure during installation
  • Inspect mandrel break and head formation
