AISI & Duplex Fasteners

Overview

AISI (American Iron and Steel Institute) and Duplex stainless steel fasteners represent the pinnacle of corrosion-resistant fastening solutions. These premium fasteners are engineered for the most demanding industrial applications where standard stainless steel simply isn't sufficient.

Our duplex fasteners combine the best properties of austenitic and ferritic stainless steels, offering superior strength, excellent corrosion resistance, and exceptional durability in harsh environments.

Material Grades

  • AISI 304 / 304L
  • AISI 316 / 316L
  • Duplex 2205 (S32205)
  • Super Duplex 2507 (S32750)
  • Duplex 2304 (S32304)

Key Benefits

  • 2x strength of 316 stainless
  • Superior chloride resistance
  • Excellent stress corrosion cracking resistance
  • High pitting resistance equivalent (PREN)
  • Cost-effective long-term solution

Technical Specifications

Tensile Strength 620-800 MPa
Yield Strength 450-550 MPa
Hardness HRC 20-32
Temp Range -50°C to 300°C
Standards ASTM A182 / A276
Surface Plain / Coated

Quality Assurance

All our AISI and Duplex fasteners undergo rigorous testing including:

  • Spectrometric chemical analysis
  • Mechanical property testing (tensile, hardness, impact)
  • Corrosion testing (salt spray, pitting resistance)
  • Dimensional inspection per ASME/ISO standards
  • Positive Material Identification (PMI)
  • Ultrasonic and dye penetrant testing
