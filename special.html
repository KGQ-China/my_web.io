Special Fasteners

Overview

Special fasteners are custom-engineered fastening solutions designed for unique applications where standard fasteners cannot meet specific requirements. Our engineering team specializes in designing and manufacturing special fasteners with non-standard dimensions, unique head styles, special materials, or specific performance characteristics.

With advanced CNC machining capabilities and extensive material expertise, we can produce special fasteners in small batches or large volumes to your exact specifications and drawings.

Customization Options

  • Non-standard dimensions
  • Custom head designs
  • Special thread forms
  • Unique drive types
  • Modified shank designs
  • Custom surface finishes

Capabilities

  • CNC precision machining
  • Cold & hot forging
  • Thread rolling & cutting
  • Heat treatment
  • Surface coating
  • Prototype to production
