Carbon Steel Fasteners

Overview

Carbon steel fasteners are the workhorse of the fastening industry, offering an optimal balance of strength, cost-effectiveness, and availability. These fasteners are used in the vast majority of industrial and construction applications where corrosion resistance is not the primary concern.

We manufacture carbon steel fasteners in various grades from 4.8 to 12.9, with a wide range of surface finishes including zinc plating, hot-dip galvanizing, and black oxide to provide varying levels of corrosion protection.

Strength Grades

  • Grade 4.8 - General purpose
  • Grade 5.8 - Structural
  • Grade 8.8 - High strength
  • Grade 10.9 - Very high strength
  • Grade 12.9 - Ultra high strength
  • SAE Grades 2, 5, 8

Finishes Available

  • Plain (oiled)
  • Zinc plated (clear/blue)
  • Yellow zinc plated
  • Hot-dip galvanized
  • Black oxide
  • Mechanical galvanizing

Surface Treatment Options

Choose the right finish for your application:

  • Plain/Oiled: Basic protection for short-term storage and indoor use
  • Zinc Plated: Good corrosion resistance for indoor and mild outdoor environments
  • Yellow Zinc: Enhanced corrosion protection with chromate conversion coating
  • Hot-Dip Galvanized: Thick zinc layer for harsh outdoor and marine environments
  • Black Oxide: Decorative finish with minimal corrosion protection
  • Mechanical Galvanizing: Uniform coating without hydrogen embrittlement risk
