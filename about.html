About Us

Company Overview

Ningbo Fasteners International Group is a leading manufacturer and exporter of high-quality metal fasteners. Founded in 1998, we have grown from a small local workshop to an internationally recognized supplier serving customers in over 60 countries.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for industries ranging from construction and automotive to aerospace and marine applications.

Our History

1998

Company Founded

Started as a small manufacturing facility in Ningbo, China, producing standard carbon steel fasteners for local market.

2005

International Expansion

Began exporting to European and North American markets. Achieved ISO 9001 certification.

2012

Product Diversification

Added stainless steel, duplex steel, and special alloy fasteners to product portfolio. Opened new manufacturing facility.

2020

Industry Leadership

Recognized as one of China's top 10 fastener exporters. Expanded titanium and super duplex product lines.

Our Mission

To provide world-class fastening solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability. We are committed to continuous improvement, sustainable manufacturing practices, and building long-term partnerships with our customers worldwide.
